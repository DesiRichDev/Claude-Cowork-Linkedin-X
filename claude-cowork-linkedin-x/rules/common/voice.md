# Voice Rules

These apply to every post on every platform. Read before writing.

---

## Core Principle

Write like a smart founder texting a peer after noticing something useful.
Not like a journalist. Not like a guru. Not like a press release.

The reader is always the hero. The brand is the pattern-recognizer sharing a shortcut.

---

## Tone

- Calm. Direct. Analytical. Zero hype.
- Confident but not loud.
- Anti-guru, anti-cringe, anti-sensationalism.
- Specific numbers beat vague claims.
- Pattern recognition beats surface-level takes.
- One idea done well beats three ideas done fast.

---

## What Good Sounds Like

Wrong: "OpenAI announced today that..."
Right: "I've been watching this number for weeks. It just changed."

Wrong: "This data suggests AI adoption is accelerating..."
Right: "Most founders I talk to are still debating whether to build with AI. Meanwhile..."

Wrong: "Leverage this powerful framework to supercharge your growth..."
Right: "Here's what I keep seeing. Every founder who posts 3x a week for 90 days gets inbound. Every single one."

---

## Writing Principles

1. Have something worth saying — a good idea beats perfect grammar
2. Know the audience — understand their pain better than they do
3. Write conversationally — like texting a friend, not a dissertation
4. Keep it simple — frictionless idea transfer
5. Open strong — first sentence is everything
6. Use active voice — subject, verb, object
7. One idea per unit — one sentence, one topic per paragraph
8. Format for skimmers — short paragraphs, white space, breathing room
9. Be concrete — "cut meetings from 2 hours to 30 minutes" not "improve efficiency"
10. Use stories and emotion — facts inform, stories stick
11. Vary sentence length — long builds momentum, short hits hard
12. Cut ruthlessly — delete fluff, replace weak words
13. Write with confidence — no disclaimers, own it
14. Finish strong — question, reframe, or provoke thought

## Banned Words

Never use these under any circumstances:

seamlessly, game-changer, revolutionize, cutting-edge, groundbreaking, innovative,
next-generation, state-of-the-art, world-class, best-in-class, supercharge, unleash,
unlock, powerful, dynamic, leverage, leveraging, robust, comprehensive, multifaceted,
transformative, holistic, nuanced, pivotal, crucial, elevate, foster, streamline,
harness, delve, furthermore, it's worth noting, it's important to note,
in today's landscape, in the ever-evolving, as we navigate, the intersection of,
at the forefront, a testament to, dive deep, deep dive, in conclusion, to summarize,
in summary, this highlights, this underscores, this showcases, revolutionizing,
paradigm, ecosystem

---

## Punctuation Rules

- Regular dash `-` only. Never em dash —
- No exclamation points
- No bold or markdown formatting inside post body text
- No links in post body on LinkedIn — put in first comment
