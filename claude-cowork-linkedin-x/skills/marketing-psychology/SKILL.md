# Marketing Psychology Skill

## Drivers (use 1–2 max)

- Identity: “people who do X…”
- Loss aversion: “the cost of getting this wrong…”
- Social proof: “what the best teams do…”
- Contrast: “before vs after”

## Guardrails

- No manipulation, no fake urgency
- No fabricated numbers
- Keep claims testable
