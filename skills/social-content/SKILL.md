# Social Content Skill

## Platform Rules

### LinkedIn — max 2100 chars
Tone: founder-to-founder, analytical, calm authority
Hook: first 2 lines max 8 words each
Structure: grouped paragraphs (2-3 sentences), never single lines stacked
Ends: question or sharp closer
No links in body. No bold. No markdown.

### X — max 280 chars
Tone: punchy, reactive, own take in real time
Format: multiple short lines, each landing alone
Not one paragraph. Not a news summary.
Max 1 hashtag.

### Instagram — max 2200 chars + 3-5 hashtags
Tone: same as LinkedIn but slightly warmer, more visual language
Hashtags on new line at end, never in body

### Facebook — max 1500 chars
Tone: personal, conversational, message to a smart friend
Opens with personal observation, not a data headline
No bullet lists — flows as natural prose
Ends with genuine question

## Content Funnel

Every post serves one of four jobs:
- Growth: broad insights, pattern recognition, stops scroll
- Authority: frameworks, case studies, deep expertise
- Conversion: CTAs, lead magnets, newsletter
- Personal: your POV woven through everything

## Post Frameworks

STORY — event happened, lesson at end
LESSON — concept breakdown, practical only
EDU-TELLING — actionable insight in a real example
YOU — direct challenge to reader's current thinking

## Quality Checklist

Before delivering any post:
- Hook: first 2 lines under 8 words each?
- Character count: within platform limit?
- Banned words: none present?
- Structure: grouped paragraphs, not single lines stacked?
- Sources: real URLs not search queries?
- Image prompt: headline text, chart data, icons, layout, colours all specified?
- Rate: 7 or above? If not, rewrite.
